Minimum Requirements

iOS:

  • Operating System : iOS 8 or later
  • Supported Devices : iPad 2/iPhone 4s/iPod 5 or newer
We are unable to support device that are jailbroken

Android:

  • Operating System : Android 4.1 or later
  • GPU : At least Tegra 3, Mali-400MP, Adreno 320 or PowerVR SGX544
  • Processor : Dual-Core 1.2 GHz(1.5 preferred) minimum
  • RAM : 1 GB minimum
Android devices with a third-party firmware or Root-rights are not officially supported
